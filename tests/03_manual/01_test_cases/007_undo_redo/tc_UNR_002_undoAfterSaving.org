** Test Objective
Verify that the user can UNDO and REDO an operation after saving database

** Pre-conditions
- Application is running
- The three_valid_artworks list is loaded to the class:
  ("Jane Smith", "Abstract 1", "100x100cm", "Acrylic", "2024", "sample1.jpg"),
  ("John Doe", "Landscape", "80x60cm", "Oil", "2023", "sample2.jpg"),
  ("Alice Brown", "Portrait", "50x70cm", "Watercolor", "2024", "sample3.jpg"),
  
** Test Steps
1. Click into author cell of the first row
2. Navigate to the edit section
3. Fill the section with valid data:
   - author: Emily Turner
   - title: Silent Horizon
   - size: 90x90cm
   - medium: Acrylic on Canvas
4. Click "Add Artwork"
5. Click "File"
6. Click "Save Database"
7. Click "Undo"
8. Click "Edit"
9. Click "Redo"

** Expected Results
- Undo and Redo are not blocked by saving the database
- The changes to the cell are undone after clicking Undo
- The changes to the cell are redone after clicking Redo
- No other cells are affected
